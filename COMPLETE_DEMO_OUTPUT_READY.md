## 🎨 Design Principles Applied

1. **Collapsed by default** - Show only headers/summaries
2. **Expand on click** - Smooth chevron animation reveals details
3. **Scannable** - Can understand workshop at a glance
4. **Color-coded** - Visual hierarchy with consistent colors
5. **Icons** - Every section has relevant emoji/icon
6. **Not overwhelming** - Elegant like PAM Wellness, not "puking words"
