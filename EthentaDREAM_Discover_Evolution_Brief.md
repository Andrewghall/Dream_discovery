# EthentaDREAM -- Discover Phase Evolution Brief

## Purpose

We need to evolve the Discover output.

Right now Discover feels like a structured summary.

That is not enough.

Discover is not data collection.\
It is a collective moment of truth for the organisation.

It must surface structural insight, not just aggregate what was said.

The output should feel like:

> "This is who we actually are."

Not:

> "Here's what was discussed."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Shift Required

Move from:

Conversation → Summary

To:

Conversation → Structured organisational analysis

Discover must expose:

-   Alignment\
-   Misalignment\
-   Tension\
-   Constraint weight\
-   Narrative divergence\
-   Confidence levels\
-   Actor friction

This is synthesis, not reporting.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Discover = Collective Synthesis of Individual Viewpoints

Discover is the phase where:

-   Individual narratives surface\
-   Assumptions collide\
-   Friction becomes visible\
-   The system reveals itself

The output must reflect this structural revelation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Discover Analysis Dashboard -- Required Components

We need a dedicated Discover Analysis dashboard, not just a summary
page.

### 1. Alignment Heatmap

Show alignment and divergence across:

Themes (e.g. Cost, CX, Risk, Speed, Quality, Compliance)\
Actors (Customer, Agent, Leader, Regulator, Operations, etc.)

Visual intensity showing:

-   Strong agreement\
-   Weak alignment\
-   Active divergence

This immediately reveals structural cohesion or fracture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 2. Tension Surface

Rank the top unresolved tensions based on:

-   Frequency of mention\
-   Strength of language\
-   Cross-actor disagreement\
-   Perceived risk weight

Clicking a tension should show:

-   Competing viewpoints\
-   Affected actors\
-   Related constraints

This must feel serious and, if necessary, uncomfortable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 3. Narrative Divergence View

Compare language patterns across:

-   Executive layer\
-   Operational layer\
-   Frontline layer

Surface where language and perception differ.

Example:

-   Exec: "Efficiency"\
-   Frontline: "Workload pressure"\
-   Ops: "Compliance risk"

This reveals hidden misalignment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 4. Constraint Map

Surface constraints mentioned and weight them:

-   Budget\
-   Regulation\
-   Legacy systems\
-   Skill gaps\
-   Risk appetite\
-   Cultural resistance

Show frequency and dependency relationships.

This becomes the foundation for Reimagine.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 5. Confidence Index

Identify areas where:

-   Language shows certainty\
-   Language shows uncertainty\
-   Hedging or ambiguity appears

This reveals maturity and knowledge gaps.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Design Principles

Discover must feel:

-   Honest\
-   Structural\
-   Calm but confronting\
-   Executive-grade\
-   Not cluttered\
-   Not like Miro\
-   Not like a word cloud

This is not brainstorming output.

It is an organisational diagnostic mirror.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Important

Do not just count themes.

We must:

-   Detect contradiction\
-   Detect polarity\
-   Detect emotional weight\
-   Detect implicit hierarchy\
-   Detect cross-actor conflict

DREAM should expose organisational structure, not just surface content.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Strategic Implication

If Discover becomes deep and structural:

-   Reimagine becomes grounded\
-   Educate becomes relevant\
-   Apply becomes justified\
-   Mobilise becomes inevitable

If Discover remains shallow, everything downstream feels conceptual.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Product Direction

We are moving from:

Summary engine

To:

Organisational truth engine.

This Discover Analysis layer is critical to that evolution.

Build for structural insight, not narrative recap.
